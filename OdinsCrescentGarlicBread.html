Odin's Godly Crescent Garlic Bread

Odin's Godly Crescent Garlic bread is the penicle of fancy. That god sure did have class!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerted crescent roll dough
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the over to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line baking sheet with Parchment paper.
  2. Mix Butter, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a bowl
  3. Open can of biscuits but do not unroll the dough. Cut dough into 12 equal pieces using a serrated knife. Place dough slices ono the prepared baking sheet and brush with
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 16 to 20 minutes.
